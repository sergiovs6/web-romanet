## Our Partnership Network

RomaNET brings together leading organizations from across Europe, each contributing unique expertise and local knowledge to our mission.

### Why Partnerships Matter

Combating hate speech requires:
- **Local Expertise**: Understanding specific contexts and challenges
- **Diverse Skills**: Combining research, activism, and technology
- **Wide Reach**: Accessing communities across multiple countries
- **Shared Resources**: Pooling knowledge and capabilities
- **Stronger Voice**: Speaking collectively for greater impact

### Our Partners

We're proud to work with organizations that share our values and commitment to fighting discrimination:

#### Spain
Spanish partners bring decades of experience working with Roma communities and advocating for rights.

#### Italy
Italian partners specialize in youth engagement and digital advocacy.

#### Greece
Greek partners excel in community mediation and dialogue facilitation.

#### Academic Partnership
Academic partners provide research rigor and evidence-based approaches.

### Partner Responsibilities

Each partner contributes to:
- Project governance and decision-making
- Implementation of work packages
- Community engagement and outreach
- Research and documentation
- Dissemination of results

We're always open to collaborations with organizations that share our mission. If you're interested in partnering with RomaNET, please contact us.
