body_en: |
  # About Kamira
  
  Kamira (FA.KA.MI.RA) is the National Federation of Roma Women's Associations in Spain. Founded to promote the rights and empowerment of Roma women, Kamira works tirelessly to combat discrimination and improve the lives of Roma communities across Spain.
  
  ## Our Work
  
  - Advocacy for Roma women's rights
  - Educational programs and workshops
  - Community support services
  - Policy development and consultation
  - Research on Roma issues in Spain
  
  ## Role in RomaNET
  
  As a Spanish partner in RomaNET, Kamira brings decades of experience working directly with Roma communities. We contribute expertise in:
  - Community engagement and outreach
  - Understanding local contexts and challenges
  - Providing support to hate speech victims
  - Training and capacity building
